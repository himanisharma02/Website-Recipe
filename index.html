Red Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 2 to 3 tbsp of olive oil
  • salt as needed
  • 3 to 4 garlic cloves
  • 1 cup tomato ripe red chopped
  • 2 onions finely chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper or red capsicum
  • 1 tbsp red chilli flakes
  • prpper freshly crushed for garnish

Instructions

  1. heat the pan with some oil and fry garlic and chilli flake
  2. add onions and saute until golden
  3. pour the puree and add salt
  4. mix well and cook until it reaches desired consistency
  5. make sure that the sauce is not watery
  6. add oregano
  7. off the stove and add pasta
  8. toss well until mixed
  9. garnish with herbs
